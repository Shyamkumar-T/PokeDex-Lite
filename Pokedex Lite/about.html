Welcome to PokéDex Lite

PokéDex Lite is a fan-made Pokémon discovery web app designed for trainers who love exploring the Pokémon universe in a clean, fast, and visually engaging way.Built with a modern frontend approach, this project combines Pokémon-inspired visuals with interactive features to create an exciting browsing experience for fans of all generations.

What You Can Do

  • Explore different Pokémon
  • Save your favourite Pokémon
  • Discover random Pokémon facts
  • Enjoy a smooth animated Pokémon-themed interface
  • Experience a lightweight and responsive design

The Idea Behind PokéDex Lite

PokéDex Lite is inspired by the nostalgic feeling of classic Pokémon adventures while maintaining a modern cartoon-style UI.The goal was to create something that feels playful, immersive, and easy to use — like stepping into the Pokémon world itself.
